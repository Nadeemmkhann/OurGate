Welcome To OurGate!
at your doorstep

OurGate is a society management application which include online society maintenance software that takes care of the daily affairs of the society. In order to reduce the responsibilities of the society members we have developed cooperative society web application. It also works as society accounting software, apartment management software and many more that will help the society to carry on its daily affairs online. The people too can be tension free because the accounts of the society will be handled by a trust worthy accounting software which is computerised.

Our Society Management System is a web application that will automate , manages and maintains all the record for visitor in an online mode. The entries of visitor are entered by the admin and the approve or reject pop-up will be generated on the user login. Flat user can pre entries the visitors for approval from the supervisor. E-Notice Board in which admin or Supervisor will announce the event or function that will be going to take place in society. Flat user can see the announcement from their respective login and can comment on the announcement. Our system will have the functionality of generating the maintenance bill automatically and bill will be mailed to flat user. Payment gateway will be provided to the flat user to pay the maintenance bill in online mode. Complaint box or issue box will be provided to all the user login
